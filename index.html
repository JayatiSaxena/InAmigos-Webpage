About InAmigos Foundation

InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8 registered non-profit organization committed to driving positive impact at the grassroots level. Established with the aim of uplifting underserved communities, we focus on long-term development through education, empowerment, and awareness programs. Our work is guided by compassion, collaboration, and a strong belief that small actions can lead to significant change in society.

Our Ongoing Projects

Project BachpanShala

Project BachpanShala focuses on providing quality education and learning support to underprivileged children. Through interactive teaching methods, digital resources, and mentorship programs, we aim to bridge the educational gap and ensure that every child has access to knowledge and growth opportunities.

Project Seva

Project Seva is dedicated to serving vulnerable communities by distributing food, clothing, and essential resources. The initiative focuses on supporting homeless individuals and economically weaker sections of society, ensuring dignity and basic human needs for all.

Project Jeev

Project Jeev works towards the rescue, care, and protection of stray and injured animals. The initiative includes feeding drives, medical assistance, and awareness campaigns to promote kindness and responsibility towards animals.

Project Udaan

Project Udaan empowers women by providing skill development training, career guidance, and self-employment opportunities. The initiative helps women become financially independent and confident in building their own futures.

Project Prakriti

Project Prakriti is focused on environmental conservation and sustainability. Through tree plantation drives, cleanliness campaigns, and awareness programs, we encourage people to take responsibility for protecting and preserving nature.

Project Vikas

Project Vikas aims to enhance youth employability through skill development workshops, career counseling, and practical training sessions. The goal is to prepare young individuals for better job opportunities and a successful future.

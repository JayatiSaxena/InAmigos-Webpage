About InAmigos Foundation

Founded in 2020, InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8 registered NGO dedicated to creating positive social impact across India. Through community-driven initiatives, the organization works towards education, women empowerment, environmental conservation, animal welfare, and humanitarian support.

Our Ongoing Projects

Project BachpanShala

Providing quality education and learning opportunities to underprivileged children.

Project Seva

Food and clothing distribution for vulnerable communities and families.

Project Jeev

Animal rescue, protection, feeding drives, and welfare initiatives.

Project Udaan

Empowering women through skill development and financial independence.

Project Prakriti

Tree plantation, sustainability drives, and environmental awareness.

Project Vikas

Skill development and employability enhancement programs for youth.
